About the Café

Café No. 15 blends premium craftsmanship with a serene garden lounge. Calm oak interiors, curated playlists, and seasonal plates set the tone for every visit.

Our Concept

Dear guests,

We are a place of relaxation.

Visitors should take their time and enjoy their stay. Both in the shop and in our beautiful garden, you are surrounded by nature. The large old trees provide welcome shade and create a peaceful place in the middle of the city.

Please leave work outside and keep laptops in your bags. Our sign “No Working Place” indicates that this café is meant for relaxing and enjoying the moment.

With our principle of slow food, we guarantee high-quality regional and fresh ingredients, some organic, prepared with care when you order.

This takes time.

We do not offer fast food or fast service. Guests who are in a hurry may not enjoy the experience.

Because we believe in avoiding food waste, sometimes dishes may sell out.

We wish you a relaxing stay and hope you enjoy your time with us. We are always happy to receive constructive feedback and improve your experience.
